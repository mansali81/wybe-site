The Complete Nutrition Guide for Your Child, From First Breath to Fifth Birthday

About the Book

The first 1825 days of a child's life represent the most nutritionally critical window of human development. What happens in that window shapes everything that follows.

Written by Mansoor Ahamad Ali Mohamed, a certified sports nutritionist with over two decades of practical experience, 1825 Days is a comprehensive, science-backed resource designed to give parents the knowledge and confidence to nourish their children from birth through the first five years of life.

The book covers every developmental stage: breastfeeding and formula guidance for newborns, the transition to solid foods, navigating toddler nutrition, building healthy eating habits before the age of five, and understanding exactly what the growing body needs at each phase. It is written with the rigour of a nutritionist and the clarity of a practitioner who works with real people every day.

There are very few resources that bridge the gap between clinical nutritional science and what parents actually need to do at home. This book is that bridge, written for parents, grandparents, and anyone entrusted with feeding a young child through their most important years.

Who It's For

New Parents

From the first days home with a newborn through the first months of feeding, this book answers the questions that matter most when everything is new and every decision feels significant.

Parents of Young Children

If your child is already eating solid foods and you want to ensure you are setting the right foundations for their health and relationship with food, this is your practical reference.

Gifting

A meaningful and lasting gift for any expecting parent or family welcoming a new child. A resource they will return to again and again across five years of growth.
