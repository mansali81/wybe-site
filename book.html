Five years that shape a lifetime.

The first 1825 days of a child's life represent the most nutritionally critical window of human development. What happens in that window shapes everything that follows.

Written by Mansoor Ahamad Ali Mohamed, a student of science, certified in Human Performance Nutrition and Personal Training, and holding a Master in Business Administration with over two decades of practical experience. The 1825 Days is a comprehensive, science-backed resource designed to give parents the knowledge and confidence to nourish their infant through the first five years of life.

The book covers every developmental stage:

  • Breastfeeding and formula guidance for newborns
  • The transition to solid foods
  • Navigating toddler nutrition
  • Building healthy eating habits before the age of five
  • Understanding exactly what the growing body needs at each phase

There are very few resources that bridge the gap between nutritional science and what parents actually need to do at home. This book is that bridge, for parents and anyone entrusted with feeding a young child through their most important years.

Who It's For

New Parents

In the early weeks and months, every feeding decision can feel uncertain. This book replaces guesswork with science-backed answers, so the choices you make are ones you can trust.

Parents of Young Children

If your child is already eating solid foods and you want to ensure you are setting the right foundations for their health and relationship with food, this is your practical reference.

Gifting

A meaningful and lasting gift for any expecting parent or family welcoming a new child. A resource they will return to again and again across five years of growth.
