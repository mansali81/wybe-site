Personal Training (1 on 1)

For those who require absolute discretion, complete personalisation, and a trainer who is fully invested in their outcome.

By Application Only

Private training in the UAE is by application only and available to a very limited number of clients at any given time. Every element of the programme, from session design to nutrition support, is built from scratch around the client.

No standard templates, no shared attention.

What to Expect

Areas of expertise

Every client’s programme is built from these foundations and shaped to their specific goal. Two decades of practical work backs every recommendation.

Strength Training

Progressive overload programming across the major lifts to build real, transferable strength.

Muscular & Cardiovascular Endurance

Conditioning work that builds work capacity without burning out the central nervous system.

Flexibility

Mobility and range-of-motion work integrated into training, not bolted on as an afterthought.

Posture Correction

Identifying and correcting compensatory patterns from desk work, training history, or daily life.

Form & Technique

Correct execution grounded in human biomechanics and kinesiology. Every lift, every rep.

Breathing for Lifting

Intra-abdominal pressure, bracing, and breath patterns that protect the spine and improve output.

Fat Loss & Body Transformation

Sustainable body composition work that pairs training intelligently with nutrition.

Special Population Training

Programming for kids, teens, geriatric clients, and those training with medical conditions.
