Intermediate

A nine-month, questionnaire-driven plan for those with a real training base, ready to progress with structure and intent.

Who It's For

Is the Intermediate plan right for you?

The Intermediate plan is built for those who already train consistently and understand the basics. You know your way around a gym, you have a foundation in the main lifts or movement patterns, and the easy gains have already come and gone.

What you need now is structure that respects your level, periodises across nine months, and pushes you beyond the plateau you have probably been sitting on for a while.

You'll Recognise Yourself If

  • You have 1–3 years of consistent training experience
  • You are competent in the main compound lifts
  • Progress has slowed or stopped on your current routine
  • You want progressive overload, periodisation, and a real diet plan
  • You are committed to nine months of intentional work
