Beginner

A six-month, questionnaire-driven workout and diet plan built for people taking their first real step into structured training.

Who It's For

Is the Beginner plan right for you?

The Beginner plan is built for those at the start of their fitness journey. If you are new to structured training, returning after a long break, or have been training inconsistently without a clear plan, this is where to begin.

You don't need experience with the gym. You don't need to know the names of exercises. What you bring is the commitment to show up for the next six months. The rest is built around you.

You'll Recognise Yourself If

  • You have 0–12 months of consistent training experience
  • You are unsure how to structure a week of training
  • Your nutrition feels random or reactive, not planned
  • You want to build base strength, mobility, and habit
  • You are ready to follow a plan for the full six months
